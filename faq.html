How is ReferBuddy associated with BitClout?

ReferBuddy is not directly associated with Bitclout. It is one of the many experiments/projects around Bitclout which is trying to enhance the onboarding experience here.

How is ReferBuddy able to sponsor this?

We are putting money from our own pocket initially. But, we strongly believe in the goodwill and the supportive nature of the Bitclout community. We will be putting the FR we receive as the reward money and we hope we get enough supporters to bring many new faces to this community.
